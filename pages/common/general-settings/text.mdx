## Add Text

You can use here to add text to your videos.

You can add many texts and determine their positions, colors, fonts and sizes individually.

### Rules

While making your settings, you can separate each text with the "::" parameter. 

For example, when you want to add two separate texts

You can parse these texts as **your first text**::**your second text**.

## Text Size

This is the area where you determine the size of the texts you add.


## Text Color

This is the section where you can adjust the color settings of your texts.


## Text Coordinates

Text positions are determined here. You can determine the location of your texts by entering X and Y coordinates.


## Text Font

If you want to add special fonts to your texts, you can use here. You can choose any of the Windows fonts on your computer.


## Text Opacity

The transparency of the texts is adjusted here. As value decreases, transparency increases.

Hit Gun is the most advanced build of the VE3.Plus system.

It enables the production of all types of content, from horizontal videos to verticals.

It has five core features called **Factory**, **Viral**, **Wide**, **Render** and **Subtitles**.

All of these features have unique sub-features within them.
